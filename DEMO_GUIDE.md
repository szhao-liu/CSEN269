## Demo Flow

### 1. **Splash Screen** (Entry Point)
- The app starts with a splash screen
- Automatically navigates to login/signup

### 2. **Authentication**
- **Login Page**: Sign in with existing account
- **Sign Up Page**: Create a new account
- Options include:
  - Email/Password authentication
  - Google Sign-In
  - Apple Sign-In (if on iOS/macOS)

### 3. **Welcome Page**
After authentication, you'll see:
- Welcome message: "Welcome to your College Prep Journey"
- Three feature cards explaining the app:
  - Track everything needed for college prep
  - Grade-specific tasks with overview of all grades
  - Trusted, carefully chosen guidance
- **"Get Started"** button (blue, bottom of screen)
- **Help button** (blue circle, bottom right)
- **About Us** button (group icon, top right)

### 4. **Choose Grade Page**
- Select your current grade level
- Each grade has specific tasks
- You can explore all grades to see the full picture

### 5. **Tasks Page**
- View and complete tasks for your selected grade
- Track your progress toward college preparation
- Upload resumes and documents

## Troubleshooting

If the app doesn't open automatically:
1. Check the terminal for any error messages
2. Look for a Chrome window that opened
3. The app should be available at `http://localhost:XXXX` (port shown in terminal)

## Stopping the Demo

To stop the app:
- Press `Ctrl+C` in the terminal, or
- Close the Chrome window and stop the Flutter process
